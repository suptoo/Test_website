Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our printing services

What are your turnaround times?

Our standard turnaround times vary by product and quantity. Most business cards, flyers, and brochures are completed within 3-5 business days after proof approval. Large format printing typically takes 2-3 business days. Rush services are available for an additional fee. Please contact us for specific turnaround times for your project.

Do you offer design services?

Yes, we offer professional design services for all types of printing projects. Our team of experienced designers can create custom designs from scratch or work with your existing materials to enhance them. Design services are billed at an hourly rate or as a flat project fee, depending on the complexity of the project.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept a variety of file formats including PDF, AI, PSD, INDD, JPG, and TIFF. For the best results, we recommend providing print-ready PDF files with at least 300 dpi resolution, CMYK color mode, and 1/8" bleed on all sides. If you're unsure about file preparation, our team is happy to assist you.

Do you offer shipping?

Yes, we offer shipping services nationwide. Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight of your order and the delivery destination. We use reliable carriers to ensure your printed materials arrive safely and on time. Local pickup is also available at our facility during business hours.

Can I see a proof before my order is printed?

We provide digital proofs for all orders before printing. This allows you to review your design, make any necessary adjustments, and approve the final version. For color-critical projects, we also offer hard copy proofs for an additional fee.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and business checks. For corporate clients, we offer net-30 payment terms upon approved credit application. A 50% deposit is required for all new clients and for orders over $1,000.
